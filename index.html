Project Vision

Architectural Concept Designed linearly along a mountain ridge, this residence is a study in landscape integration. The structure follows the natural contours of the terrain, blurring the line between the built environment and the rugged mountain profile.

Ground Level | Social Core The heart of the home is a central core housing the kitchen and living room, designed for fluid transition. It extends out to a panoramic lookout platform on one side and an expansive pool area on the other. This level also accommodates a private bedroom and a dedicated office for a balanced live-work experience.

Lower Level | Secluded Retreat Carved into the mountain slope, the lower level is partially subterranean, offering natural thermal insulation and privacy. It features two additional bedrooms and a media lounge integrated directly into the terrain.

Outdoor Living The design is completed by a series of outdoor tiered spaces, including a pool with an integrated tub and a dedicated BBQ area, all positioned to capture the essence of the surrounding landscape.

The goal of this portfolio is to provide a spatial map of the design intent, serving as the blueprint for a high-end cinematic architectural visualization.
